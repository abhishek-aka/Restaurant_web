We welcome you and greet you to our Sliet Restaurant. Sliet Restaurant was established in 2001. Here at Sliet Restaurant, we serve quality and quantity food with pocket-friendly prices, excellent temperature control, presentation, and top-quality service. Pricing and customer satisfaction are things we focus on and constantly improve. We provide both AC and non-AC seating arrangements. We are a multi-cuisine restaurant with a wide range of food options. Our staff ensures that you get what you want and pays extra attention to your needs. Sliet Restaurant is perfect for people who love great food.
