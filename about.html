Our Story

Founded in the heart of the SLIET Campus, our restaurant offers a cozy environment for students, faculty, and visitors alike.
Established to provide affordable yet delicious meals, SLIET Restaurant has become a popular spot to enjoy both local and international cuisine.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer high-quality food made with fresh ingredients, while fostering a community spirit on campus.
We believe that great food brings people together, and we are committed to making every meal a memorable experience for you.

What Makes Us Special?

  • Fresh Ingredients: All our dishes are prepared using locally-sourced, fresh ingredients.
  • Varied Menu: From traditional Indian dishes to continental favorites, our menu has something for everyone.
  • Student-Friendly Prices: We offer budget-friendly options without compromising on taste.
  • Events & Specials: We host regular food festivals, theme nights, and special discounts for students.

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of talented chefs, friendly staff, and hardworking students who are passionate about delivering the best dining experience on campus.
From our chefs to our service team, we are here to make your time at SLIET Restaurant special.
